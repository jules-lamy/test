Alexandre's practice focuses mainly on white collar crime and general criminal law.

He specialises in corporate criminal law (fraud, breach of trust, money laundering), employment criminal law (workplace accidents, harassment), European arrest warrants and extradition, as well as corruption offences and other breaches of probity.

Alexandre also advises on criminal offences relating to the transport of goods (illegal transport of goods, human trafficking, road transport regulations, serious traffic accidents) and on vehicle impoundment and seizure.

As a French-Polish national, Alexandre is registered on the list of lawyers of the Polish Embassy in Paris. He regularly assists and represents Polish companies and nationals throughout the French territory.

He is equally at ease handling cases in French, English and Polish.

Before co-founding ARP AVOCATS, Alexandre worked for 5 years at GUTKES AVOCATS, during which time he was involved in a number of complex cases.

Alexandre was admitted to the Paris Bar in 2019.
